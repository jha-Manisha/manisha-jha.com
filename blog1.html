Communication Skills

"A key to success"

September , 2021

"Technical Skills can get you job but a good Communication Skills let you stand out in the crowd......."

Communication skill is one of a key to success , yes - it is true because in today's world or generation , good and effective communication skills can get you confidence with a positive as well as impressive attitude on yourself in every field. Since, communication skills are an " art " that can be learned and can be developed. Developing your communication skills can help all aspects of your life from professional life to personal life and everything in-betweens like social gatherings , schools , colleges , at home etc..

So , basically , communication is one of the important tools that help us to connect with people. It is enables us to pass information to other people and also to understand what is said to us from them

The communication skills include four main components :  Listening  Speaking  Writing  Observing Basically , communication skills can be categorised as :  Verbal communication - method of expressing ourself through words and sounds.  Non-Verbal communication - these can be described as conveying a message without using any written or verbal communication.  Written communication - these are writing formats , e-mails , instructions , memos , letters , articles , documentation , reports etc.  Visual communication
